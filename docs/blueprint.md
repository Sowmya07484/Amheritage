# **App Name**: Heritage Sprint

## Core Features:

- Endless Frontier Engine: A high-performance 3D runner mechanics module with responsive swipe controls for lanes, jumping, and sliding through iconic American landscapes.
- Patriotic Quiz Tool: An AI-powered question generator tool that creates contextually relevant multiple-choice history and culture questions based on the player's current level.
- Thematic Era Progression: Dynamic level transitions that change characters and environmental aesthetics every 5 levels, moving from Founders to Action Heroes.
- Heart & Stamina Database: A persistent data system that manages life regeneration timers, coin balances, and badge collections using a real-time database.
- Freedom Power-ups: Activatable power-up systems including the Liberty Shield and Knowledge Magnet, with unique visual effects and gameplay modifiers.
- Global Valor Leaderboard: Social competitive ranking system that tracks high scores, daily streaks, and accuracy percentages globally.
- Timed Heart Regeneration: Automated server-side logic that calculates heart replenishment at 20-minute intervals and persists status across sessions.

## Style Guidelines:

- The palette centers on a heroic Primary Blue (#2563EB) and a vibrant Accent Red (#EF4444), symbolizing energy and national pride.
- A deep, desaturated Dark Indigo background (#0F172A) provides a modern 'Night Mode' aesthetic that makes interactive elements pop with high contrast.
- Pairing 'Space Grotesk' for technical HUD elements and headlines with 'Inter' for clear, legible body text and quiz questions.
- The dashboard follows a modern AAA mobile hierarchy, featuring persistent progress bars at the top and a three-lane runner focus in the central viewport.
- Custom vector icons for Power-ups and badges with a futuristic, clean-line patriotic theme including stars and silhouettes of monuments.
- Energetic UI transitions for quiz entry and heart recovery, using sleek easing for a premium gaming feel.
