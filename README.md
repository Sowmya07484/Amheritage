# American Heritage Run 🇺🇸

An educational and patriotic endless runner game built with Next.js, React, and GenAI. Sprint through history, collect coins, and master American heritage challenges to advance your campaign.

## 🎮 Game Features

### Core Mechanics
- **3D Patriotic Run**: Navigate a dynamic 3D road environment using responsive lane-switching controls.
- **Coin-Driven Challenges**: Every 10 coins collected triggers a high-stakes "Heritage Quiz".
- **Dynamic Speed**: The game gets faster as you progress through higher phases.
- **Distance Tracking**: Monitor your campaign progress with a live meter at the top of the screen.

### Character Roster & Store
- **Donald Trump**: The lead patriot, featuring iconic power suit and signature look.
- **George Washington**: Unlock the founding father for 300 coins.
- **Benjamin Franklin**: Master the polymath for 300 coins.
- **Persistent Progress**: Your unlocked heroes and coin count are saved across sessions.

### Campaign Progression
- **10 Strategic Phases**: Levels range from Geography and Founding Fathers to Expert Heritage Challenges.
- **Star Rating System**:
  - ⭐️ **1 Star (Good)**: 6-7 correct answers.
  - ⭐️⭐️ **2 Stars (Nice)**: 8-9 correct answers. Required to unlock the next level!
  - ⭐️⭐️⭐️ **3 Stars (Excellent)**: 10/10 perfect score.
- **Patriotic Celebrations**: Red, White, and Blue confetti for correct answers and perfect level completions.

## 🛠 Technical Stack

- **Framework**: [Next.js 15](https://nextjs.org/) (App Router)
- **UI Components**: [Shadcn UI](https://ui.shadcn.com/) & [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/)
- **AI Engine**: [Genkit](https://firebase.google.com/docs/genkit) & Gemini 1.5 Flash for dynamic question generation.
- **Visuals**: HTML5 Canvas for the 3D world and `canvas-confetti` for celebrations.
- **Icons**: [Lucide React](https://lucide.dev/)

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Install dependencies**: `npm install`
2. **Run the development server**: `npm run dev`
3. **Open the game**: Navigate to `http://localhost:9002`
